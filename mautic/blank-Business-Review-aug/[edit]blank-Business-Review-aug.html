HLC area now available in the library

 
 

We’ve gotten a lot of feedback from spread and arbitrage traders that our charts needed an upgrade. As a result, we’ve added a new chart type: HLC area charts. This chart type was created for advanced traders who want to visualize price spreads and more.

 

HLC area is based on three price values: High, Low, and Close. It allows traders to focus on the trading range of an asset rather than the current price. For traders with shorter trading timeframes, this new chart type gives them an entirely new way to analyze markets.

Global Stock Heatmap Widget

 
 

Our Global Stock Heatmap Widget displays a snapshot of the stock market, including the individual stocks in the market, colored and sorted by their performance. The Stock Heatmap can be segmented in a number of ways including:

 

  • Sector
  • Country
  • Market cap

 

Customize this new widget with a ton of filters in the widget constructor and enjoy!
